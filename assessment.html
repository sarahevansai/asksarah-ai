Step 5 of 7 — Citation Quality
When your brand gets mentioned in press or publications — where do those links land?
Homepage links tell AI you exist. Deep links tell AI what you're an authority on. The difference determines whether you get cited as a brand or as an expert.
Step 6 of 7 — A New Content Structure
Have you published an AI Notice?
An AI Notice isn't a press release. It's not a blog post. It's a structured, authored piece of content written specifically to be cited inside AI-generated answers — distributed through channels LLMs actively scrape: wire services, high-authority publications, LinkedIn. The goal isn't a human reader. The goal is the model.
Step 7 of 7 — The 28-Day Window
When did your brand last appear in an external publication, press outlet, or high-authority website?
AI engines weight recency. A consistent presence — at least every 28 days — keeps you in the model's active consideration. A gap longer than that starts to decay your visibility, even if your content is solid.
