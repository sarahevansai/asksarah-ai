# askSarah.ai — Strategy

## Purpose
Personal brand home for Sarah Evans. Top-of-funnel content hub that drives engagement and converts to Zen Media sales.

## Funnel
1. **Attract** — Free AI/visibility content, GPT tips, Claude workflows, AI tool guides, GEO playbooks
2. **Engage** — "Ask Sarah" AI chatbot, PR@ctical newsletter, The Visibility Equation show
3. **Convert** — Warm leads → Zen Media services (Published Monthly, GEO strategy, consulting, training)

## Content Pillars
- GPT prompts for PR/comms professionals
- Claude workflow tips (research, writing, strategy)
- GEO guides (how to get your brand into AI answers)
- AI tool reviews & comparisons
- "AI Visibility Playbook" — signature framework
- Mini-trainings → upsell to paid workshops/Stan Store

## Revenue Paths
- **Zen Media retainers** (~$15k/mo avg) — primary goal
- **Stan Store** — trainings, workshops, digital assets ($800K personal target)
- **Speaking** — keynotes and corporate workshops
- **Newsletter sponsorships** — PR@ctical (future)

## Chatbot Vision
- Train on: newsletter archive, show transcripts, LinkedIn posts, Published Monthly methodology, GEO frameworks
- Goal: give real value in conversation, then route to appropriate offer
- Backend: LLM with RAG on Sarah's content corpus
