> 🧠 **CHATBOT BRAIN: MCP education for comms — USB analogy, crisis/exec visibility scenarios, governance implications**

"'What is MCP, and do I need to know about it?' Yes. And now.

MCP = Model Context Protocol. Open standard by Anthropic defining how AI models connect to external tools, data, and services.

**USB analogy:** Before USB, every device had its own proprietary connector. After USB, one plug worked everywhere. MCP does the same for AI.

**Scenario 1 — Crisis Response:** A story drops Monday morning. With MCP, Claude reads the article, pulls your messaging framework from Google Drive, scans overnight emails, checks Slack — then drafts a holding statement, response, and talking points. All in your voice.

**Scenario 2 — Executive Visibility:** CEO has a keynote. Claude pulls recent media coverage, reviews executive quotes, checks competitor messaging, cross-references positioning doc. Surfaces gaps, flags inconsistencies, drafts a narrative audit.

**What's missing from most MCP conversations:**
- **Governance** — who controls what AI agents can access?
- **Consistency at scale** — single source-of-truth messaging
- **Institutional memory** — AI as connective tissue when people leave"
