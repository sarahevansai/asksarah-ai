How It Works

4-layer verification on every source

🔍
Layer 1
Deep Research
Runs a real-time search across news publications, trade outlets, podcast transcripts, conference speaker lists, LinkedIn, published bylines, and op-eds. Scans hundreds of sources to find the handful of people who've actually spoken publicly on your specific topic.
💬
Layer 2
Quote Verification
Pulls direct quotes from interviews, articles, podcasts, and posts. Not bios. Not LinkedIn headlines. Their actual words on your topic — with publication name, date, and context so you can verify immediately.
🤖
Layer 3
AI Visibility Check
Checks whether ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude already cite this person as an authority. If multiple AI models independently name someone as an expert, that's a credibility signal you can't fake. Also flags emerging voices AI hasn't picked up yet — your exclusive.
📧
Layer 4
Reachability
Timezone, contact pathway, recent activity, broadcast experience, and media-readiness signals. Includes response likelihood estimates and pre-written outreach emails and LinkedIn DMs so you can reach out immediately.
