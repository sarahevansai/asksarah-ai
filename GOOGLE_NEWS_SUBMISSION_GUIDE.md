# Google Publisher Center Submission Guide for askSarah.ai

This guide provides step-by-step instructions to register and submit **askSarah.ai** to the Google Publisher Center. This is the final manual step to ensure your daily AI briefings and playbooks are instantly indexed and showcased across **Google News**, the Google News App, and News-related search tabs.

---

## 📋 Pre-submission Checklist

Before you begin, ensure you have:
1. **Google Search Console access** for the domain `https://asksarah.ai` (required to instantly verify ownership).
2. **Logo Assets** ready to upload (see the asset specifications section below).
3. **RSS Feed URL** verified and live: `https://asksarah.ai/briefings.xml`
4. **Google News Sitemap** verified and live: `https://asksarah.ai/sitemap-news.xml`

---

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Publisher Center Onboarding

### Step 1: Add a Publication
1. Navigate to the [Google Publisher Center](https://publishercenter.google.com/).
2. Log in using the same Google Account that owns the Google Search Console property for `asksarah.ai`.
3. Click **Add Publication** (or **Add new**).
4. Enter the details:
   - **Publication Name**: `askSarah.ai`
   - **Primary Language**: `English (United States)`
   - **Location**: `United States`
   - **Primary Website Property URL**: `https://asksarah.ai`
5. Click **Save**.

### Step 2: Configure General Settings
1. On your publication dashboard, click **Google News** -> **Edit**.
2. Under **Basic Information**:
   - **Category**: Select `Technology` or `Business`.
   - **Distribution**: Keep as `Worldwide` (Default).
   - **Google Analytics**: Add your Measurement ID (if active) to track clicks from Google News.
3. Under **Visual Styles** (Left menu or top tab):
   - Upload your logo assets (see [Logo Specifications](#-logo-specifications) below).
4. Click **Save** in the top right.

### Step 3: Set Up Content Sections (RSS Feed Ingestion)
Google News displays content by pulling live articles from your RSS feed.
1. In the edit section, go to the **Content Settings** tab.
2. Click **New Section** and choose **Feed**.
3. Fill in the Section Details:
   - **Section Title**: `Daily Briefings`
   - **Feed URL**: `https://asksarah.ai/briefings.xml`
   - **View Access**: `Anyone` (Default).
4. Click **Add**.
5. Repeat for any industry-specific playbooks (or leave as a single feed for overall rankings).
6. Click **Save**.

### Step 4: Verify Domain Ownership
1. Go to **Organization Settings** (accessible via the gear icon or sidebar).
2. Look for the **Verified URL** status under asksarah.ai.
3. Because you are logged in as the Search Console owner, this should automatically verify. If not, click **Verify** to authenticate instantly.

### Step 5: Review & Publish
1. Go to the **Review and Publish** tab.
2. If the Publisher Center says "Empty Section" under your feed, click the **Refresh** button on your Content Section. *(Note: It may take up to 10-15 minutes for Google's crawler to parse the feed for the first time).*
3. Under **Google News publishing status**, fill in any missing contact information.
4. Click **Publish**. Google will review your publication (typically takes 2 to 5 business days). Once approved, you are officially live!

## 🎨 Logo Specifications

Google Publisher Center requires specific logos to render correctly on light and dark interfaces inside the Google News app:

1. **Square Logo / Icon** (Required)
   - **Format**: PNG or JPEG
   - **Dimensions**: `512 x 512` pixels (Recommended)
   - **File to use**: Already built and available at `https://asksarah.ai/icon-512.png` or `https://asksarah.ai/sarah-avatar.png`

2. **Rectangular Logo** (Required)
   - **Format**: Transparent PNG
   - **Height**: Must be exactly `20px` to `40px` high
   - **Width**: Must be between `200px` and `400px` wide
   - **Light Theme Logo**: Design suitable for light backgrounds (dark text/colors).
   - **Dark Theme Logo**: Design suitable for dark backgrounds (light/white text).
