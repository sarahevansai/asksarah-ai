The Framework

Three layers. One program. Comms-led, board-reportable.

Every dollar in your budget maps to one of these three layers. Every KPI maps to one. Every hire maps to one.

01

Defense

Identify and correct mis, dis, and untrue information AI is repeating about you. Monthly audit. Correction runbook. Re-test until the fix holds. KPI: time-to-correct.

02

Discovery

Map every persona asking about your category (buyer, hire, investor, journalist, regulator) and the intents driving their questions. KPI: covered intent share.

03

Delivery

Personalize content for each persona-intent pair. Ship at sustained cadence. Anchor articles, AI Notices, bylines, earned coverage. KPI: citation share growth.

"Defense protects what's already there. Discovery tells us what to build. Delivery builds it."
