The Before Layer™
The AI-generated answer people see before they click any link. Your brand's new homepage that exists whether you manage it or not.
Sarah Evans Term
Answer Share™
A composite data grouping measuring the probability of AI citation — combining domain authority, AI citation frequency, indexed mentions, and earned media presence.
Sarah Evans Term
Dual Pathway PR
The framework combining traditional earned media practice with GEO, AEO, and modern AI visibility. Two simultaneous strategies: the human pathway and the AI pathway.
Sarah Evans Term
AI Notices
The citations, attributions, and brand mentions inside AI-generated answers — earned media's newest and most under-tracked impression category.
Named by Gab Ferree
WireScore™
A rubric-based scoring system measuring whether a message will survive AI interpretation — every point deducted references a specific check, not AI vibes.
